About TeamBeat

Collaborative boards that bring teams together

What is TeamBeat?

TeamBeat is a real-time collaborative board application designed to help teams organize ideas, conduct retrospectives, and make decisions together. Whether you're running a sprint retrospective, brainstorming session, or planning meeting, TeamBeat provides the tools you need to collaborate effectively.

Key Features

Real-Time Collaboration

See changes as they happen. Every card edit, vote, and board update is instantly synchronized across all connected team members. No more refreshing pages or wondering if your changes saved.

Flexible Board Structure

  • Customizable Columns: Create boards that match your workflow
  • Multiple Scenes: Organize different phases of your session
  • Card Grouping: Cluster related ideas together

Smart Voting System

Make decisions with confidence using our built-in voting system. Facilitators can allocate votes per user and control when voting opens and closes. Results update in real-time as team members vote.

Privacy and Control

  • Blame-Free Mode: Hide card authors to encourage honest feedback
  • Role-Based Access: Admins, facilitators, and members each have appropriate permissions
  • Private Series: Keep your team's discussions secure

Perfect For

Agile Teams

Run effective retrospectives with dedicated phases for reflection, discussion, and action planning. Use voting to prioritize improvements and track progress over time.

Product Teams

Brainstorm features, prioritize user stories, and make product decisions collaboratively. Export results for documentation and follow-up.

Remote Teams

Bridge the gap between in-person and remote collaboration. Everyone participates equally, regardless of location or timezone.

Workshops and Facilitation

Guide groups through structured activities with scene management, timer controls, and real-time engagement tracking.

Design Philosophy

TeamBeat is built on the principle that collaboration should be effortless. We believe in:

  • Simplicity: Clean, intuitive interfaces that don't get in the way
  • Reliability: Robust real-time features that work when you need them
  • Focus: Purpose-built for team collaboration, not a generic tool
  • Privacy: Your team's discussions stay with your team

Getting Started

Ready to transform how your team collaborates? Here's how to get started:

  1. Create an Account: Sign up with your email or use WebAuthn for passwordless login
  2. Start a Board Series: Organize related boards under a single series
  3. Invite Your Team: Add team members with appropriate roles
  4. Create Your First Board: Choose from templates or build custom layouts
  5. Collaborate: Start adding cards, voting, and making decisions together

Built for Teams

TeamBeat scales with your needs:

  • Small Teams: Perfect for startup teams and small projects
  • Department-Wide: Support multiple concurrent sessions
  • Enterprise Ready: Robust architecture designed for scale
